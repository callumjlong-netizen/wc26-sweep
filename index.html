How to Play

To enter the sweepstake see Dean Tatchell or Curt Newton. Entries cost £5 each.

You can buy multiple entries to increase your chances.

1

Draw 4 teams

Draw 4 numbers from the bag at random. Each number corresponds to one of the 48 World Cup teams listed in group stage order. No duplicate numbers.

2

Draw 4 players

Draw 4 numbers from the bag at random. Each number corresponds to a squad player number MAX 26 redraw if above.

The first number drawn is the player for your first team, the second for your second team, and so on. No duplicate numbers.

If the number drawn is not an available player number for that team's squad, the next lowest available player number will be chosen.

3

Check your picks

Make note of your numbers and use the Squads page to verify your team and player picks.

Rules & Scoring

🏆At the end of the tournament the prize pot will be split between the entries in the 1st, 2nd, 3rd, and last position as a percentage of the total prize pot. 60%, 25%, 10%, and 5% respectively - Check the leaderboard for the current positions and prize amounts.
⚽Points are scored when your team scores a goal or your player scores a goal.
+1Each goal scored by your team adds 1 point to your total.
+XEach goal scored by your player adds points based on their position multiplier:
PositionMultiplierExample
Forward×11 goal = 1 point
Midfielder×21 goal = 2 points
Defender×41 goal = 4 points
Goalkeeper×61 goal = 6 points
🚫Goals scored in a penalty shootout do not count. Only goals scored during normal time and extra time are awarded points.
🚫Player positions are determined by each country's official FIFA squad list submission confirmed on the 2nd June.
