Rules & Scoring

🏆At the end of the tournament the prize pot will be split between the entries in 1st, 2nd, 3rd, and last position — 60%, 25%, 10%, and 5% of the total pot. Check the leaderboard for current positions and prize amounts.
⚽Points are scored when your team scores a goal or your player scores a goal.
+1Each goal scored by your team adds 1 point to your total.
+XEach goal scored by your player adds points based on their position multiplier:
PositionMultiplierExample
Forward×11 goal = 1 point
Midfielder×21 goal = 2 points
Defender×41 goal = 4 points
Goalkeeper×61 goal = 6 points
🚫Goals scored in a penalty shootout do not count. Only goals scored during normal time and extra time are awarded points.
📋Player positions are determined by each country's official FIFA squad list submission confirmed on the 2nd June.
